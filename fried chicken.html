Fried Chicken

Ingredients:
  • 250 gm chicken
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • salt as required
  • 2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoon paprika
  • black pepper as required
Step-by-Step Recipe:
  1. To begin with, this delicious recipe, wash the chicken properly with lukewarm water
  2. Put the buttermilk in a bowl and dip chicken pieces in buttermilk for few times
  3. Keep it aside for a few minutes. Make sure the flour gets a paste-like consistency. Meanwhile, take a large pan and heat vegetable oil on a high flame
  4. When chicken turns brown in colour, cover the pan with a lid and reduce the heat. Cook the chicken on medium flame
